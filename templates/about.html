Inclusive Learning Assistant

An advanced AI-powered platform engineered to ensure inclusive access to educational materials for all learners, with particular emphasis on students with diverse accessibility needs.

Project Overview

The Inclusive Learning Assistant is a comprehensive final-year academic initiative that harnesses Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing to develop accessible learning experiences for students with diverse needs.

Our platform provides intelligent tools including Text-to-Speech synthesis, Speech-to-Text recognition, AI-powered content summarization, and multi-language support to ensure every student can access and comprehend educational materials in their preferred format.

Mission Statement

To bridge the digital accessibility gap in modern education and empower students with disabilities through innovative, AI-driven solutions that foster inclusive learning environments.

Core Features

Text-to-Speech Synthesis

Convert written content to natural-sounding audio

Speech-to-Text Recognition

Transcribe spoken words to written text

Document Processing

Extract and process PDF, DOCX, and PPTX files

AI Summarization

Generate concise summaries of lengthy content

Accessibility Standards

WCAG compliant design for all users

Multi-Language Support

Content translation across multiple languages
